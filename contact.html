Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by clicking the "Track via WhatsApp" button on our homepage, or by contacting us directly on WhatsApp with your order details.

What are your delivery times?

We typically deliver within 3-7 business days across South Africa. Local deliveries in Thohoyandou area are usually completed within 1-2 days.

Do you accept returns?

Yes, we accept returns within 14 days of purchase, provided items are in original condition with tags attached.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, cash on delivery, and WhatsApp orders. All payment details will be provided after order confirmation.

How do I know what size to order?

Each product page includes size information. If you're unsure, feel free to contact us on WhatsApp for personalized sizing advice.
